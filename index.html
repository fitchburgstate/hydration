Hydration Games

Fall 2019 Game Studio Capstone
Faculty: Britt Snyder & Jeff Warmouth

LORE

A 1-4 player 2D couch coop beat em up that emphasizes teamwork and combos. Each player will play a set of unique characters based on 4 different roles and battle against enemies, mini bosses and a boss at the end.

By Melido J. Feliz Perez, Nolan Harris, Aurom Lum, Jay Ortiz, and Nicholas St Jacques

Play on Itch.io

Neo Inferno

A high-octane arcade style stationary action shooter set in a modern day interpretation of Hell. The player takes command of an agent whose out to find his missing partner somewhere in the vast underworld. Will he unearth her location and defeat the demons who stand between him and his objective?

By Alex DiOrio, Maxim Frolov, Marissa Tautiva, and Austin Winkler

Play on Itch.io

Tile Heroes

An isometric RPG where you create the adventure by placing down the path tile by tile. Finding party members, fighting monsters, and acquiring items to become strong enough to fight the boss monster at the end of the quest.

By Christopher Cornford, Brockton DiSalle, and Zachary Jesionowski

Play on Itch.io
